When the brief needs more detail

Some Brisbane commercial spaces need a deeper reset, more detailed hygiene work, or a one-off clean before handover, inspection, tenant change, reopening, or a busy trading period.

Specialised cleaning is useful when the usual weekly clean is not enough. That might be a commercial kitchen that needs extra detail, a tenancy handover, a high-touch hygiene refresh, or a space that has been busy for months and needs to be brought back to standard.

We clarify the scope before the job so expectations, access, and timing are practical from the start.

Specialised work can include

  • High-touch point disinfecting and hygiene refreshes
  • End-of-lease or pre-occupancy commercial cleaning
  • Deep cleaning for kitchens, staff areas, wet areas, and shared facilities

Specialised cleaning scope in more detail

Specialised cleaning is usually tied to a specific outcome: handover, hygiene, inspection readiness, post-event recovery, or a reset after a busy period. The scope should be clear enough that everyone knows what is included before work starts.

Handover and reset cleaning

Useful when a tenancy, office, shop, or shared space needs to be brought back to a cleaner standard before the next stage.

  • End-of-lease, pre-occupancy, pre-opening, and post-fit-out commercial cleaning.
  • Detailed attention to kitchens, staff rooms, amenities, reception spaces, and visible surfaces.
  • Dust, marks, fingerprints, cupboard fronts, splashbacks, benches, and general presentation detail.
  • One-off site resets before moving into a regular commercial cleaning schedule.

Hygiene and high-touch focus

For sites that need extra care around shared contact points, busy staff areas, or customer-facing spaces.

  • Door handles, switches, rails, counters, shared equipment, tapware, and common touchpoints.
  • Staff kitchen surfaces, appliance surrounds, sinks, bins, wet areas, and shared break spaces.
  • Clinic, office, retail, and education spaces that need a deeper periodic hygiene refresh.
  • Practical scheduling around trading hours, staff access, and building requirements.

What helps us quote specialised cleaning

Detailed jobs are easier to quote when the outcome is clear. A few photos and practical notes usually make the first conversation much more useful.

  • The reason for the clean: handover, inspection, hygiene, post-event, pre-opening, or reset.
  • Rooms and areas to include, plus anything that should be excluded.
  • Photos of the current condition and any areas that need extra attention.
  • Access times, deadline, parking, keys, alarms, and building entry requirements.
  • Whether floors, windows, kitchens, amenities, or high-touch points need separate attention.
  • Any site rules around products, noise, security, waste, or working outside business hours.

Specialised cleaning FAQs

Can you quote from photos?

For many one-off jobs, clear photos and a short description are enough to start. Larger or more detailed jobs may need a site check.

Do you do end-of-lease commercial cleaning?

Yes. We can help with commercial handover cleans, including staff areas, amenities, kitchens, floors, and presentation areas.

Can specialised cleaning become a regular service?

Yes. If the site needs ongoing support, we can turn a one-off clean into a regular commercial cleaning plan.

Share the space, timing, and outcome you need so we can respond clearly.
