Frequently asked questions

Using a mortgage broker is generally free of charge for home buyers. We are paid via a commission by the lender you choose to settle your loan with. Our recommendations are strictly based on your best interest requirements.

Discussing your options and running calculations does not impact your credit score. Hard credit enquiries are only lodged when we officially submit a formal application to a lender on your behalf.

The pre-approval process usually takes anywhere from 3 to 14 business days, depending on the complexity of your situation and the current turnaround times of the chosen lender.
